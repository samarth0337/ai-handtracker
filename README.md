An interactive, browser-based Augmented Reality (AR) experience that utilizes advanced real-time hand tracking. Built entirely with vanilla web technologies, this project transforms your webcam feed into a highly responsive, neon-infused digital playground featuring particle physics, dynamic audio synthesis, and striking visual aesthetics.

## ✨ Features

### 👁️ Advanced Real-Time Tracking
* **MediaPipe Integration:** Smooth, low-latency tracking for up to two hands simultaneously.
* **Dynamic HUD:** Real-time on-screen telemetry displaying FPS, Hand Count, Gesture Recognition (Fist, Open Hand, Pinch), and Hand Spread Percentage.

### 🎨 Visual Effects & Physics Engine
* **Matrix Rain Background:** A digital rain overlay that dynamically speeds up based on your hand movement velocity.
* **Neon Bloom & Particle Systems:** Glowing fingertip trackers that emit trailing particles and sparks.
* **Interactive Geometry:** * Draws adaptive, colored gradients connecting your hands.
  * Generates electric lightning arcs when fingertips are brought close together.
  * Forms a slow-rotating geometric Mandala when all 10 fingers are detected.
* **Thematic Customization:** 5 instantly switchable color profiles: `Rainbow`, `Cyberpunk`, `Lava`, `Ocean`, and `Galaxy`.

### 🎵 Procedural Web Audio
* **Theremin-Style Audio:** A continuous background hum that modulates in frequency (pitch) and volume based on the physical distance between your left and right index fingers.
* **Shockwave Zaps:** Pinching your thumb and index finger triggers an expanding visual shockwave and a synthesized "zap" sound effect.
